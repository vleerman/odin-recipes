Yakitori

Description

Yakitori are japanese skewered chicken thigh with spring onions in between. It tastes great with yakitori sauce and will be the highlight on the BBQ.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sake
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
    • <1i>1 piece fresh ginger root, grated
  • 1 pound chicken thigh, cubed
  • 6 spring onions, one per skewer

Steps

  1. In a medium dish, mix together the sake, soy sauce, sugar, garlic and ginger. Add chicken, marinate for 15min.
  2. Grease six metal skewers. Thread alternately with chicken and spring onion. Brush with the marinade
  3. Broil for 5 minutes on the BBQ, baste and broil for another 5 minutes
